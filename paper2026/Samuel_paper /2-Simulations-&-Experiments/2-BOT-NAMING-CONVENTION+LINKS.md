**BOT NAMING CONVENTION**

Based on our experiment design, we can use an intuitive naming
convention that directly reflects the key aspects: regulation status and
personality type :

- **Personality Type A:**

  - Bots with Regulation: **A-Reg-1, A-Reg-2, \..., A-Reg-6**

  - Bots without Regulation (Baseline): **A-Base-1, A-Base-2, \...,
    A-Base-6**

- **Personality Type B:**

  - Bots with Regulation: **B-Reg-1, B-Reg-2, \..., B-Reg-6**

  - Bots without Regulation (Baseline): **B-Base-1, B-Base-2, \...,
    B-Base-6**

This naming convention makes it easy to identify:

- The personality type being tested: **A** or **B**

- Whether the bot has regulation: **Reg** (regulated) or **Base**
  (baseline, unregulated)

- The specific bot: **1** through **6**
