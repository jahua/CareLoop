## Writing Style Improvements

### Replace Bullet Points with Prose

**Current:**
```
**Human Expert Panel**:
- Two PhD-level in-house research experts
- Expert 1: PhD in Clinical Psychology, 14 years
- Expert 2: PhD in Computer Science + clinical minor, 10 years
- Both experts have ≥3 years professional experience
- Complementary perspectives: clinical + technical
```

**Improved:**
```
We assembled a two-person expert panel with complementary expertise: one 
PhD-level researcher with clinical psychology training and 14 years in 
mental health interventions and healthcare AI evaluation, partnered with 
another PhD holder in computer science with clinical psychology training 
and 10 years experience in healthcare AI systems. Both experts bring ≥3 years 
of professional experience specifically evaluating AI-generated outputs and 
algorithmic bias in clinical contexts, ensuring both psychological validity 
and technical rigor in the validation process.
```

### Key Principles:
✅ Topic sentence first (establishes what section accomplishes)
✅ Supporting details follow naturally  
✅ Transition words between ideas
✅ Active voice ("We assembled..." not "An expert panel was assembled...")
✅ Concise but flowing language
