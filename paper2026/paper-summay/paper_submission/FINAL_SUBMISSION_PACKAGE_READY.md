## 💡 PRO TIPS

### For Figure Embedding:
- **Consistent sizing:** Keep all figures at ~6 inches width
- **Don't compress:** Word may ask to compress images - click "No" to maintain quality
- **Center alignment:** All figures should be centered
- **Keep captions:** Don't delete the caption text below each figure

### For MDPI Submission:
- **Double submission:** Upload manuscript with embedded figures AND separate PNG files
- **File naming:** MDPI will ask you to label each figure (Figure 1, Figure 2, etc.)
- **Check preview:** MDPI portal shows how reviewers will see your manuscript
- **Save drafts:** You can save and return to the submission form

### For Author Information:
- **Corresponding author:** Mark clearly with asterisk (*)
- **Equal contribution:** Note if authors contributed equally
- **Current addresses:** Use current institutional affiliations
- **ORCID IDs:** Highly recommended (register at orcid.org if you don't have one)
