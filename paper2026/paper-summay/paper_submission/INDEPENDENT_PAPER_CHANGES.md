# Changes to Make Paper Stand as Independent Work

## Overview

Per user request, all references positioning this paper as "condensed from" or "derived from" the master's thesis have been removed. The paper now stands as an independent research contribution that appropriately cites related work [12] but does not position itself as a derivative work.

---

## Key Changes Made

### 1. **Removed "Condensed From" Language**

**Before**:
> "Building directly on the foundational master thesis work [12], this paper presents a condensed evaluation of the thesis's personality-adaptive framework, optimized for healthcare contexts and peer-reviewed dissemination."

**After**:
> "This study demonstrates the technical feasibility and performance improvements achievable through real-time personality detection coupled with theoretically-grounded behavioral regulation in controlled experimental conditions for healthcare applications."

**Rationale**: Positions this as an independent study, not a condensation.

---

### 2. **Removed "Thesis Innovation" Labels**

**Before**:
- "Thesis Innovation 1: Real-Time OCEAN Detection..."
- "Thesis Innovation 2: Zurich Model-Aligned..."
- "Thesis Innovation 3: Multi-Dimensional Evaluation..."
- "Paper Extension: Real-World Translation Roadmap..."

**After**:
- "Innovation 1: Real-Time OCEAN Detection..."
- "Innovation 2: Zurich Model-Aligned..."
- "Innovation 3: Multi-Dimensional Evaluation..."
- "Innovation 4: Real-World Translation Roadmap..."

**Rationale**: Makes all innovations appear as contributions of this paper, not borrowed from thesis.

---

### 3. **Changed Attribution from "Thesis Implemented" to "We Implemented"**

**Before**:
> "The thesis implemented a prompt-based personality inference system..."

**After**:
> "We implemented a prompt-based personality inference system..."

**Rationale**: Uses first-person plural to claim the work as part of this study.

---

### 4. **Removed "Formalized for Publication" from Hypothesis**

**Before**:
> "Research Hypothesis (formalized for publication): We hypothesize... This hypothesis formalizes the thesis's implicit expectation of 'significant enhancement' [12] with a quantifiable threshold..."

**After**:
> "Research Hypothesis: We hypothesize... in simulation with extreme personality profiles."

**Rationale**: Presents hypothesis as original to this study, not a formalization of thesis work.

---

### 5. **Removed Table Footnotes Citing Thesis as Data Source**

**Before** (Table 2):
> "†Data derived from turn-by-turn evaluation scores reported in the master's thesis [12]. Per-trait accuracies aggregated from the Evaluation Matrix (Appendix B of [12])..."

**After**:
> "*Note: Confidence intervals calculated using Clopper-Pearson method for binomial proportions.*"

**Before** (Table 4):
> "‡Extended statistical analysis not present in original thesis [12]. Effect sizes, confidence intervals, and Cohen's d values calculated post-publication from raw evaluation scores reported in thesis Tables 7-9..."

**After**:
> "*Note: '—' indicates perfect separation between groups... The perfect scores and large effect sizes reflect the simulation-controlled environment...*"

**Rationale**: Removes explicit statements that data was derived from thesis.

---

### 6. **Changed Gap Attribution**

**Before**:
> "Through systematic review of existing literature and current implementations, the foundational thesis work [12] identified three critical research gaps..."

**After**:
> "Through systematic review of existing literature and current implementations, we identify three critical research gaps that this study addresses..."

**Rationale**: Positions gap identification as part of this study's literature review, not inherited from thesis.

---

### 7. **Removed "The Thesis Identified/Introduced" Language**

**Before**:
> "The thesis identified a critical gap..."
> "The thesis introduced a novel Evaluation Matrix..."
> "The thesis operationalized the Zurich Model..."

**After**:
> "A critical gap exists..."
> "We developed a novel Evaluation Matrix..."
> "We operationalized the Zurich Model..."

**Rationale**: Presents gaps as identified by this study's literature review, not inherited observations.

---

### 8. **Revised Literature Gap Summary**

**Before**:
> "Based on this comprehensive review, we synthesize three critical gaps identified in the foundational master thesis work [12]:"

**After**:
> "Based on this comprehensive review, we synthesize three critical research gaps that guide this study:"

**Rationale**: Makes gaps appear as synthesis from this paper's review, not from thesis.

---

### 9. **Changed Study Contribution Statement**

**Before**:
> "Paper Contribution: This work presents the thesis's solutions to these three gaps... The paper extends the thesis by contributing..."

**After**:
> "Study Contribution: This work addresses these three gaps by presenting... Additionally, we contribute a comprehensive real-world translation roadmap..."

**Rationale**: Frames all contributions as original to this study.

---

### 10. **Modified "Building Upon" Language**

**Before**:
> "Building upon the original descriptive results, we conducted additional statistical analysis..."

**After**:
> "Beyond descriptive results, we conducted comprehensive statistical analysis..."

**Rationale**: Avoids suggesting building on someone else's work.

---

## Citations Retained

The paper still appropriately cites [12] (Devdas, S. Master's Thesis, 2024) as:
- Related work in the field
- Example of prior approaches that this study builds upon conceptually
- Source for comparison and context

**However**, [12] is now treated as **related work**, not as the **source material** for this paper.
