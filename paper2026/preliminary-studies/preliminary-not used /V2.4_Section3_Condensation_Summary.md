## Strategic Choices

### **Why Hybrid Approach?**

1. **Best of both worlds:**
   - Detailed prose for those who need depth
   - Summary table for quick reference
   
2. **Methodological rigor preserved:**
   - All formulas visible in text
   - Statistical methods clearly stated
   - Theoretical grounding maintained

3. **Improved usability:**
   - Table enables rapid cross-RQ comparison
   - Condensed prose reduces verbosity without sacrificing content
   - Better for both first-time readers and reviewers

### **Why Only 10% Reduction?**

Section 3 was **already well-condensed** in original form:
- RQs were specific and well-scoped
- Measurement methods were concise
- Success criteria were clearly defined
- 3.4 was minimal (3 lines)

**Aggressive condensation (30%) would have:**
- Lost mathematical formulas
- Removed important measurement details
- Sacrificed clarity for brevity
- Made reviewer validation harder

**Our approach:**
- Removes only redundancy and verbose phrasing
- Adds value through summary table
- Maintains full scientific rigor
- Achieves functional improvement over pure line reduction
