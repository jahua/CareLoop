✅ Engagement Formula (Week 10)
   - Data-driven logistic regression (50 human-rated examples)
   - Cross-validation: AUC≥0.75
   - Replace arbitrary weights (+0.5, +0.75) with empirical ones

✅ Stress Level Calibration (Week 10)
   - 100 synthetic examples, human-anchored scale (0-4)
   - Confusion matrix + κ per level
   - Target: κ≥0.70 per level, κ≥0.75 overall

✅ Temporal Dynamics Analysis (Weeks 15-16)
   - Vector Autoregression (VAR) model
   - Granger causality: stress→engagement lag effects
   - Demonstrates temporal causality, not just correlation

✅ Balanced Baseline Comparisons (Weeks 11-14)

   - All 4 systems scored on same 5 criteria by human experts
