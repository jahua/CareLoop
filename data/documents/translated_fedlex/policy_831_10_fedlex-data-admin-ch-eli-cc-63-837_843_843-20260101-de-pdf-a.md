# A. General

Art.49 General Principle

The implementation of old-age and survivors' insurance is carried out under the supervision of the Confederation (Art.76 ATSG244) by employers and employees, as well as by association equalization funds, cantonal equalization funds, federal equalization funds, and a central equalization office.

Art.49a Information Systems and Requirements

1. The implementing bodies operate information systems that enable electronic exchange of information and data processing.

2. They ensure that their information systems have the necessary stability and adaptability at all times, as well as information security and data protection.

3. The professional organizations of the implementing bodies develop rules for implementing the requirements of Article 72a Paragraph 2 Letter.

Art.49b Information Systems for the Implementation of International Agreements

The Federal Council can require the implementing bodies to use information systems developed for fulfilling the tasks under Annex II of the Agreement of June 21, 1999248 between the Swiss Confederation on the one hand and the European Community and its Member States on the other hand concerning the free movement of persons (Free Movement Agreement), as well as other international agreements on social security, and after hearing the affected parties.

# Art.49c Register of Ongoing Financial Benefits

1. The central equalization office under Article 71 keeps a central register of ongoing financial benefits, including available information on the granting of foreign benefits, for the purpose of:

   a. preventing the receipt of unlawful financial benefits;
   b. creating transparency about the granted financial benefits;
   c. adjusting the financial benefits.

2. It includes:

   a. ongoing financial benefits;
   b. deaths and civil status changes of entitled persons, and if available, the date of birth of the spouse or registered partner.

3. It reports deaths and civil status changes to the equalization funds and provides the necessary data to the authorities under Article 50b Paragraph 1.

Art.49d Insured Register

1. The central equalization office keeps a central insured register for the purpose of:

   a. assigning an AHV number to insured persons under Article 50c;
   b. ensuring that in the event of a pension, all individual accounts of a person are taken into account.

2. It includes:

   a. insured persons and their AHV number;
   b. equalization funds that maintain an individual account for an insured person;
   c. foreign insured numbers necessary for the implementation of international social insurance agreements.

3. It provides the necessary data to the authorities under Articles 50b Paragraph 1 and 153c Paragraph 1.
