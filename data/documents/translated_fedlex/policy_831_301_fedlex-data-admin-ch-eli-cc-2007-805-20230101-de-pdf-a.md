Ordinance on the Federal Contribution to Premium Reduction in Health Insurance (VPVK) of November 7, 2007 (as of January 1, 2023)
The Swiss Federal Council, based on Articles 66 and 96 of the Federal Act of March 18, 19941 on Health Insurance (KVG), ordains:
Art. 1 Subject Matter
This Ordinance regulates:
a. the determination of the federal contribution to premium reduction in accordance with Article 66 KVG;
b. its allocation to the cantons.
Art. 2 Gross Costs
1 The gross costs in accordance with Article 66 Paragraph 2 KVG are calculated based on the following key figures:
a. Average premium (P);
b. Insured population (V);
c. estimated insured population (Vest);
d. Premium claim (PS);
e. Cost participation (KB).
2 The average premium (P) corresponds to the monthly average premium for adults aged 26 and over for mandatory health insurance with regular franchise and accident coverage. The Federal Office of Public Health (BAG) calculates it based on the approved premiums of individuals residing in Switzerland by cantons and premium regions.
3 The insured population (V) corresponds to the average number of the following insured persons during the relevant year:
a. Insured persons residing or staying in Switzerland; and

Art. 3 Allocation to the Cantons
1 The share of each canton in the federal contribution is calculated based on the following key figures:
a. Resident population of the canton (BevK);
b. Resident population of Switzerland (BevCH);
c. Number of cross-border commuters and their family members according to Article 65a letter a KVG in the canton (GrK);
d. Number of cross-border commuters and their family members according to Article 65a letter a KVG in Switzerland (GrCH).
